Fire Fit — Privacy Policy

Effective date: October 11, 2025

Who we are

Fire Fit is a private fitness tracking app operated by the owner (“we”, “us”). If you have questions, contact owner@starlit-peony-ea4d6c.netlify.app.

Information we collect

  • Account info: name, email, sign-in provider (via Firebase Authentication).
  • Profile data: display name, role, tier (stored in Firestore under /profiles/{uid}).
  • Progress data: standards checkoffs and history (/profiles/{uid}/checkoffs), weekly logs (/weeklyChallenges/{weekId}/logs).
  • Technical data: basic app logs and aggregated usage metrics (non-identifying).

How we use data

  • Authenticate users and manage roles.
  • Display member progress, checkoffs, and weekly challenges.
  • Maintain security and improve app performance.

Legal basis

We process data to provide the service you request (contract) and for our legitimate interests in operating, securing, and improving the app.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Data is stored and processed by our service providers (e.g., Google Firebase) to run the app.

Data retention

We keep data while your account is active. You may request deletion at any time—see our User Data Deletion page.

Your rights

  • Access, update, or request deletion of your data.
  • Object to or restrict processing where applicable.

Contact us at owner@starlit-peony-ea4d6c.netlify.app.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures, including Firebase security rules and role-based access controls.

Children

Fire Fit is not intended for children under 13. If we learn we have data from a child under 13, we will delete it.

Changes

We may update this policy. We will post the new version on this page with an updated effective date.
