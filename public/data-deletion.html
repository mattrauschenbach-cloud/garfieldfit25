Fire Fit — User Data Deletion

Fire Fit respects your privacy. This page explains how to request deletion of your account and associated data.

What data we hold

  • Authentication profile (name, email, sign-in provider)
  • Member profile stored in Firestore (/profiles/{uid})
  • Checkoffs and history (/profiles/{uid}/checkoffs)
  • Weekly challenge logs (/weeklyChallenges/{weekId}/logs where uid matches your account)

How to request deletion

You can request deletion in either way below:

  1. Email request (recommended): Send an email to owner@starlit-peony-ea4d6c.netlify.app from the same email you used to sign in, with the subject "Fire Fit — Delete my account".
  2. In-app request: If available in your account settings, use the “Delete my account” option. (If you don’t see this option, use the email method above.)

We will delete your data within 30 days of verifying the request.

What gets deleted

  • Your Firebase Auth user
  • Your profile document and subcollections (checkoffs, history)
  • Your weekly logs

Aggregated or anonymized analytics (if any) are not personally identifiable and may be retained.

Contact

For any questions, contact: owner@starlit-peony-ea4d6c.netlify.app
